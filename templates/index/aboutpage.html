About Ailixir Global Limited

Ailixir Global is an innovative pharmaceutical and biotechnology company focused on merging Artificial Intelligence (AI) with modern and traditional medicine. Our goal is to create intelligent, data-driven solutions that revolutionize drug discovery, improve healthcare accessibility, and integrate Africa’s rich herbal knowledge into global health innovation.

At Ailixir Global, we harness the power of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science to revolutionize the future of healthcare. Our mission is to accelerate drug discovery, predict treatment outcomes, and optimize herbal formulations through intelligent algorithms.

Rooted in the wisdom of traditional African medicine and enhanced by cutting-edge technology, we create safer, faster, and smarter healthcare solutions tailored for the modern world.

By bridging the gap between nature and innovation, Ailixir is redefining what's possible in pharmaceutical research and personalized treatment.

Our vision is to be the world’s leading AI-driven medical innovation company originatingfrom Africa.

Innovation Focus

Ailixir focuses on developing AI algorithms to accelerate drug discovery, predict treatment outcomes, and optimize herbal formulations using data science. By combining traditional African medicine with AI precision, we aim to produce safer, faster, and smarter healthcare solutions.

Divisions & Operations
  • 1. Ailixir AI – Development of machine learning models for health analytics.
  • 2. Ailixir Labs – Research and development of novel drugs and formulations.
  • 3. Ailixir Bio – Production and biotechnological innovations.
  • 4. Ailixir Access – Improving medicine distribution in underserved areas.
  • 5. Ailixir Foundation – Promoting health education and AI awareness.
